Disclosures

1. Financial Disclosures

Your Pharmacy is committed to transparency in its financial dealings. We disclose any financial relationships with third parties, including but not limited to manufacturers, wholesalers, and suppliers. These relationships are structured to maintain independence and to serve the best interest of our customers.

  • We do not receive compensation from drug manufacturers in exchange for favoring specific products.
  • Any discounts or rebates we receive from suppliers do not influence our prescription filling processes or recommendations.

2. Product Disclaimers

All medical and pharmaceutical products offered by Your Pharmacy are regulated by relevant health authorities. However, no product is guaranteed to work for every individual, and patients should consult their healthcare provider before starting any new medication. The following disclaimers apply:

  • Medications and supplements are dispensed as per prescription and are not guaranteed to be effective for all users.
  • We are not responsible for any adverse reactions or outcomes resulting from the misuse of medications.

3. Conflict of Interest Disclosures

At Your Pharmacy, we are committed to operating with integrity. We disclose any potential conflicts of interest that may affect our business operations. This includes financial interests, affiliations with medical institutions, or ownership stakes in related industries.

  • Our pharmacists are not permitted to have financial relationships with healthcare providers or pharmaceutical companies that would influence their professional judgment.
  • We disclose any ownership or partnership interests that our business may hold in other healthcare-related companies.

4. Professional Affiliations and Endorsements

We are proud to work with various professional organizations to stay informed about best practices in pharmacy care. However, we do not endorse any specific healthcare providers, products, or services unless explicitly stated. All affiliations are disclosed in our public reports to ensure transparency.

5. Health Information Disclosures

While Your Pharmacy provides health-related content and information, this should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice. We encourage all patients to seek advice from their healthcare providers before making any decisions about medications or treatments.

  • The information provided on our website is for educational purposes only.
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of third-party health information linked from our site.

6. Regulatory Compliance

Our pharmacy operates in strict compliance with all local, state, and federal regulations governing pharmaceutical dispensing, data privacy, and consumer protection. We continually update our practices to reflect new laws and guidelines to ensure the safety and satisfaction of our customers.

  • We comply with HIPAA regulations to protect the privacy of your personal health information.
  • We adhere to FDA regulations on the dispensing and sale of prescription medications.

7. Advertising and Sponsorship

Any advertising or sponsorship on our website will be clearly identified as such. Our pharmacy does not allow paid advertisements to influence the content or recommendations found on our platform. All advertising relationships are disclosed in accordance with applicable laws.

  • We do not accept advertising from companies whose products are inconsistent with our mission of improving patient health and safety.
  • Any sponsored content or partnerships are clearly marked to distinguish from editorial content.

8. Changes to Our Disclosures

We may update this disclosure page periodically to reflect changes in our business operations, partnerships, or regulatory environment. Any significant changes will be communicated via our website and updated promptly on this page.
